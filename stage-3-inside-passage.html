Stage III: Inside Passage

WA, BC & AK • 2008-2009

Overview

1,400 miles by kayak through the legendary Inside Passage from Washington through British Columbia to Alaska. Navigating sheltered waters between the mainland and coastal islands, this stage followed one of North America's most spectacular maritime routes through fjords, narrows, and remote coastal wilderness.
