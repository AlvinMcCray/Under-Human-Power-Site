Stage I: Pacific Crest Trail

Mexico to Canada • 2004-2005

Overview

2,650 miles through California, Oregon, and Washington. Beginning at the Mexican border in April 2004, the journey followed the spine of the Sierra Nevada and Cascade ranges, crossing deserts, alpine passes, and volcanic peaks before reaching the Canadian border in September 2005.

The highest point on the Pacific Crest Trail. Snow-covered slopes, thin air, and spectacular views across the High Sierra. The pinnacle moment of the entire route.
