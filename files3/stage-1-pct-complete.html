Stage I: Pacific Crest Trail

CA, OR & WA • 2004-2005

Overview

2,650 miles from the Mexican border to Canada, following the spine of the Sierra Nevada and Cascade mountain ranges through California, Oregon, and Washington. Beginning in April 2004 at Campo, California, the route traversed desert, alpine passes, volcanic peaks, and endless forests before reaching Manning Park, British Columbia in September 2005.
