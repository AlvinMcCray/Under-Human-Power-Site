Stages V & VI: Brooks Range & Arctic Ocean

Alaska • 2011

Overview

The final 500 miles of the expedition. From Ruby, the route led overland through Alaska's Brooks Range—the northernmost mountain range in North America—before descending to the Arctic Ocean at Prudhoe Bay. This ultimate stage crossed some of the most remote and challenging terrain on the continent, completing a seven-year journey from Mexico to the Arctic Circle.
