The Expedition

From the sun-scorched Mexican border to the frozen reaches of the Arctic Circle, Darrell J. Gardner embarked on an unprecedented journey powered by nothing but human determination.

Following the Pacific Coast Highway, threading through the Inside Passage of British Columbia, and pushing north beyond the edge of civilization—all by hiking, kayaking, and sheer willpower.
